About Us

Tejshree Apartments

A convenient all new apartment community with access to all Tejshree Apartment has to offer at an approachable price point. Adjacent grocery and numerous neighborhood amenities, within a short drive or bike to downtown Tejshree Apartment as well as quick easy access to I-88 and all of Tejshree Apartment major employers. From the sophisticated and modern interiors, to the inviting, intelligently-curated amenity spaces, Vantage Tejshree Apartment is suburban living elevated and refined. It is ideal for the way you live and all at an approachable rental price point.

This Apartment with Scandinavian inspired interiors consists of 2 Bedrooms, 2 Bathrooms, Full Kitchen and Living/Dining Area. Backed up by solar-power, this apartment is in Thamel, Kathmandu. Next door to great nightlife, restaurants, pubs/bars, shopping & entertainment.

In over 5 years long history, Tejshree Apartments, Nepal first opened its door in the ever-developing Sanepa Road. Designed by the famous Irish artist, Desmond Doig we can still feel his presence in the design of the garden, the paintings, and the halls.

Conveniently located at the heart of the capital, Kathmandu, Tejshree Apartments is a great base from which to explore this vibrant city. At Tejshree Apartment, the traditional touch in the hotel’s interiors fusion with state of the art facilities never fail to make our guests stay worthwhile. Further complemented by other prominent features of the hotel include 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi in all public areas, 24-hour security, wheelchair accessible, daily housekeeping service.
